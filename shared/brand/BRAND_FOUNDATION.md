# Tumtum Brand Foundation

## Brand Purpose

Tumtum exists to prove that the most powerful moments in life leave a mark — not just in memory, but in the body. We capture the invisible thread between a heartbeat and a moment of pure emotion, turning fleeting feelings into something tangible, shareable, and unforgettable. Beyond technology, Tumtum celebrates the human experience of being moved by music, sports, and collective energy.

## Brand Vision

To become the universal language of live emotion — a world where every concert, match, and festival produces a personal, biometric story that people treasure and share as naturally as a photo or video. Tumtum will redefine how fans remember, relive, and connect through the moments that made their hearts race.

## Brand Mission

Tumtum captures real-time heart rate data during live events — concerts, sports matches, and festivals — and transforms it into beautiful, shareable visual stories. We serve passionate fans in Brazil and beyond who crave a deeper, more personal way to relive the moments that moved them most.

## Brand Values

1. **Emotion First**: Every feature, design choice, and interaction must amplify the emotional connection between a person and their experience. Data is never just data — it is a feeling made visible. We prioritize what moves people over what merely impresses them.

2. **Radical Simplicity**: Capturing a heartbeat story should be as effortless as showing up to the event. We remove friction obsessively — from wearable sync to card sharing — so the technology disappears and only the experience remains.

3. **Trust & Privacy**: Heart rate is intimate, personal data. We collect only what is necessary, give users full control, communicate transparently about data usage, and never compromise user trust for growth. Privacy is not a feature; it is a foundation.

4. **Community & Connection**: Live events are collective experiences. Tumtum strengthens the bonds between fans, between fans and artists, and between strangers who shared the same peak moment. We build bridges through shared heartbeats.

5. **Bold Authenticity**: We celebrate real reactions — the genuine spike during a last-minute goal, the sustained rush of an encore. Tumtum never fabricates or exaggerates. The truth of a heartbeat is powerful enough.

## Brand Personality

- **Electric**: Tumtum feels alive, energetic, and charged — like the moment right before the lights go down at a show. Our visuals pulse, our animations breathe, and our language carries the intensity of a live crowd.

- **Intimate**: Despite the energy, Tumtum speaks to you personally. Your heartbeat, your peaks, your story. The brand creates a feeling of closeness — like a whispered "did you feel that too?" between friends at a concert.

- **Nocturnal & Premium**: Tumtum lives in the night — dark interfaces, glowing reds and cyans, the aesthetic of stage lights cutting through darkness. The brand feels premium without being exclusive, sophisticated without being cold.

- **Playful & Shareable**: At its core, Tumtum is fun. Comparing heartbeats, discovering your peak moment, seeing how in sync you were with an artist — these are delightful, social experiences. The brand encourages sharing with infectious enthusiasm.

- **Confident**: Tumtum knows what it is. The brand communicates with clarity and conviction, avoiding jargon or over-explanation. Short sentences. Bold visuals. No apologies.

## Brand Promise

When you bring Tumtum to a live event, you will walk away with a stunning, personal record of exactly how that experience made you feel — captured in your heartbeat, beautifully visualized, and ready to share with the world. Every beat tells your story.

## Tone of Voice

- **Language**: Portuguese (Brazil) as default for all user-facing content, with English i18n ready.
- **Register**: Conversational but polished. Like a friend who works in creative direction.
- **Do**: Use short, punchy sentences. Speak in second person ("your heartbeat", "your moment"). Evoke sensory experiences. Use music and sports metaphors naturally.
- **Don't**: Use clinical health/medical language. Over-explain the technology. Sound corporate or detached. Use emojis excessively — let the visuals do the talking.

## Target Audience

**Primary**: Young adults (18-35) in Brazil, specifically Sao Paulo, who attend concerts and football matches regularly. They are active on Instagram, TikTok, and X. They already wear smartwatches or fitness bands. They love sharing experiences on social media and are drawn to visually rich, personal content.

**Secondary**: Artists, athletes, and event organizers who see Tumtum as a new way to engage with their audience and understand crowd energy.
